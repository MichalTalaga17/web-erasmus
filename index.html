About The Suski District

The Suski District is located in The Beskidy Mountains, on south-west part of Lesser Poland. From west it borders with The Żywiecki District in Upper Silesia, from north and east with other districts Lesser Poland: Wadowicki, Myślenicki and Nowotarski, from south with The Namiestovo District in Slovakia.

Entertainment in the region

There is something suitable for everyone

  • Farther surroundings

The area of ​​the Suski District and its surroundings are among the best developed tourist places in Poland. Spending time here, you can climb the mountains, swim in the lakes and visit one of the largest amusement parks in Europe.

Castle of Sucha Beskidzka

Renaissance Castle in Sucha Beskidzka, called "Little Wawel".

Tavern "Rzym"

Tavern "Rzym" is one of the last examples of great wooden architecture of folk culture in Lesser Poland.

Castle Park and orangery

The Park is adjacent to the castle and the woods on the Jasień Mountain. In the second half of the nineteenth century the neo-gothic orangery and the flower garden were built in the Park.

Monastic complex of regular canons

The construction of the church was completed in 1614. 10 years later, the construction of the abbey began - today it houses a rectory and a complex of seven chapels. In 1987 - 1908 a new church was built, which It was later connected with a one-story lacing with a separate place for the sacristy with the historical one part of the complex.

Zawoja

Babia Góra 38km from Sucha Beskidzka

Majestatic and dominating part of landscape. Everyone wonders about its beauty and mystery . . .

Maków Podhalański

Sanctuary of Our Lady of Makowska 8km from Sucha Beskidzka

In the center of Maków Podhalański there is the Church of the Transfiguration. From 1590 in the main altar of the temple houses a venerated image of the Virgin Mary.

Zawoja

Open air-museum Józefa Żaka 24km od Suchej

It was built in 1973 near Babiogórski National Park.

Jordanów

Main Square in Jordanów 23km from Sucha Beskidzka

Wars, occupation, fires ravage this city, but luckily, it have stayed almost in perfect condition. We can still walk the XVI century streets.

Zator

Energylandia Amusement Park 37km from Sucha Beskidzka

The largest amusement park in Poland. You can have fun regardless your age. Park offers entertainment for childen, teenagers and adults.

Inwałd

Inwałd Park 31km from Sucha Beskidzka

It is a complex of 5 theme parks. You can visit Paris, meet T-Rex, experience medieval habits, travel around a wonderful lands by a train.

Wadowice

Wadowice 23km from Sucha Beskidzka

It's a small town, but known really well by all Poles, due to beloved pope John Paul II, who was born and spent his childhood there.

Kalwaria Zebrzydowska

Monastery of the Bernardine Fathers 23km from Sucha Beskidzka

Kalwaria is a bustling place of worship, visit by 2 million pilgrims every year. They are attracted to Paths of Jesus and Paths of our Lady and the Miraculous Image Mary of Calvary.
